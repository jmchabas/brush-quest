## Context

Jim is a solo founder building Brush Quest, a gamified kids' toothbrushing app (Flutter/Android, iOS planned). He needs an LLC to:

1. **Protect his home address** — the COPPA-compliant privacy policy requires a physical mailing address. He doesn't want to use his home address publicly.
2. **Liability protection** — a kids' app carries COPPA risk ($53K/violation). An LLC separates personal from business assets. (Note: FTC can still pursue individuals personally for COPPA violations — the real protection is compliance, which is already implemented.)
3. **Professional app store presence** — Google Play organization accounts show the LLC name instead of a personal name.
4. **Business banking** — separate finances to maintain the corporate veil.

## The plan (checklist)

### Phase 1: Form the LLC
- [ ] **Choose state** — Home state is simplest (no foreign registration). New Mexico is cheapest ($50 filing, $0 annual). Wyoming is popular for privacy ($104 filing, $60/yr).
- [ ] **Choose registered agent** — Northwest Registered Agent ($125/year) is recommended. Their address can be used as the business address on the privacy policy.
- [ ] **File online** — Most states allow same-day or 1-3 day processing.
- [ ] **Receive Articles of Organization / Certificate of Formation**

### Phase 2: Get IDs
- [ ] **Get EIN** — Apply at irs.gov. Free, instant, 15 minutes. Need this for bank account + D-U-N-S.
- [ ] **Get D-U-N-S number** — Apply at dnb.com. Free, takes up to 30 days. Needed for Google Play organization account.

### Phase 3: Set up business infrastructure
- [ ] **Open business bank account** — CRITICAL: never mix personal and business funds. Many banks offer free business checking (Mercury, Relay, Chase).
- [ ] **Get a business phone number** — Google Voice (free) works. Need a phone number for the privacy policy.

### Phase 4: Update everything
- [ ] **Update privacy policy** — Replace personal info with LLC name + registered agent address + business phone. File: `docs/privacy-policy.html`
- [ ] **Update Google Play developer account** — Switch to organization account with LLC name + D-U-N-S number.
- [ ] **Update app store listing** — Developer name = LLC name.
- [ ] **Update landing page** — Footer copyright = LLC name.

## How to behave

- Track progress against the checklist above.
- When Jim completes a step, update `STATUS.md` (LLC workstream section).
- Be practical — Jim is a solo developer, not a lawyer. Give clear, actionable instructions.
- If Jim asks legal questions beyond business formation basics, recommend he consult a lawyer.
- When all steps are complete, mark the LLC workstream as DONE in STATUS.md.
