# Trademark + Bundle ID Conflict Search — Brush Quest

> Searched 2026-04-28. Re-run before any future name change or before filing a USPTO trademark application.

## Verdict

**Submission risk from name "Brush Quest" itself: LOW.**
**Submission risk from existing iOS App Store namesake: MITIGATED via subtitle.**

We can use "Brush Quest" without trademark conflict. The single real risk was an existing iOS app with the same name; we're addressing it by submitting under the title **"Brush Quest: Space Rangers"** to differentiate.

## Searches performed

### 1. USPTO TESS — manual browser search at https://tmsearch.uspto.gov

Search query: `brush quest` (Wordmark search, default partial-match).

| Filter | Live results | Exact "BRUSH QUEST" wordmark? |
|--------|-------------:|------------------------------|
| All Classes, Live | 1,566 | None (DOM scan via Chrome MCP find tool) |
| IC 009 (Computer Software) Live | 901 | **None** |
| IC 041 (Education & Entertainment Services) Live | 642 | **None** |

The 1,566 Live results are all single-word matches for "BRUSH" or "QUEST" or compound marks like "BUSTER BRUSH", "BRUSH & BARLEY", "BOOK BRUSH", "BRUSH CRAZY", etc. None combine the two words.

**Conclusion:** No registered or pending USPTO trademark for "BRUSH QUEST" in IC 009 or IC 041 as of 2026-04-28.

### 2. Apple App Store search

Existing app: **Brush Quest** by **Mitchell Pothitakis** — published 2025-07-18, Health & Fitness category. Gamified toothbrushing for kids 4-10 with character companions (Dragon/Unicorn/Robot).

- Listing: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/brush-quest/id6748761829
- Bundle ID (their app): `com.mitchellpothitakis.BrushQuest`
- Bundle ID (our app): `com.brushquest.brushQuest` — **distinct**, no technical collision

**Mitigation:** Submit under title "Brush Quest: Space Rangers" to clearly differentiate at the App Store search level. Apple Review Guideline 4.1 (Copycats) risk drops substantially with the subtitle.

### 3. Google Play Store search

No existing Android app titled "Brush Quest" in the public store (besides our own internal-test build, which isn't publicly indexed).

### 4. Domain conflicts

- `brushquest.app` — owned by AnemosGP LLC ✓
- `brushquest.com` — squatted (NameBright registrar, expires 2026-06-07). Not pursuing.

## Recommendations

1. **Ship as "Brush Quest: Space Rangers" on iOS.** Differentiates from the existing iOS app, no extra cost, leverages existing "Space Rangers vs Cavity Monsters" branding.
2. **Keep Android title as "Brush Quest: Kids Toothbrush"** (already live). No conflict on Google Play, no need to rename.
3. **Consider filing a USPTO trademark for "BRUSH QUEST"** in IC 009 + IC 041 — ~$250–$1,500 + 6–12 months. Not a launch blocker, but worth considering once the app has revenue or strategic value, to lock in priority over Pothitakis. Defer until Phase 4+.
4. **Re-run this search annually**, especially before any title change or international expansion.

Open https://tmsearch.uspto.gov, type `brush quest` in the search box, click the search icon. Filter sidebar: uncheck "Dead", check IC 009 and IC 041 in the Class filter. Scan results for any wordmark exactly equal to "BRUSH QUEST" (with or without space/hyphen).
