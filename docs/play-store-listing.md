## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

```
Your kid hates brushing their teeth. You've tried bribery, countdown timers, and that toothbrush shaped like a dinosaur. Every night is the same negotiation.

Brush Quest turns that fight into something your child actually asks for.

Your child becomes a Space Ranger. The only way to attack Cavity Monsters? Brushing. Every stroke powers their hero's weapon. No reading required — just tap, brush, and enjoy.

HOW IT WORKS

Your child picks a hero, taps start, and a voice-guided countdown begins. The app walks them through 6 mouth zones — 20 seconds each — while their hero battles a Cavity Monster on screen.
A mouth diagram shows exactly where to brush. Voice prompts guide every step. Your child never needs to read a word.
After 2 minutes: victory!

WHAT KEEPS KIDS COMING BACK

6 heroes that evolve — kids brush to unlock the next form. 10 brushing worlds with unique monsters, from Candy Crater to Crystal Cave. And finally Streaks that encourage regular brushing.

Kids don't brush because you told them to. They brush because they want to evolve Blaze into a Flame Knight and unlock the next world.

DESIGNED FOR PARENTS

• Dentist-recommended across 6 zones
• Adjustable zone timing (15, 20, or 30 seconds) to match your child's age
• No ads. No in-app purchases.
• Fully voice-guided, designed for ages 3–10
• Optional camera motion — real brushing powers in-game attacks (on-device only, never recorded)
• Cloud sync with Google sign-in
• COPPA compliant — no tracking, no ad networks, no data sold

Built by a dad in California who was tired of the nightly brushing battles :-) Battle-tested by his 7-year-old and 3-year-old - Now they ask to brush.

Questions or feedback? I read every email - Thank you!
```
