# Google Play Store Listing — Brush Quest

## App Name
Brush Quest: Kids Brushing Game

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Defeat cavity monsters while brushing your teeth! A fun 2-min game for kids.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Turn toothbrushing into an epic space adventure! In Brush Quest, your child becomes a Space Ranger battling cavity monsters across 10 exciting worlds.

HOW IT WORKS
- Start a 2-minute brushing session with the big BRUSH button
- Follow the visual mouth guide showing which teeth to brush
- Attack cavity monsters by brushing — the more you move, the harder you hit!
- Defeat the monster before time runs out to earn stars

COLLECT & UNLOCK
- 6 unique Space Ranger heroes with special powers
- 6 powerful weapons to equip
- 70 monster cards to collect across 10 worlds
- Daily login rewards and streak bonuses
- Surprise reward chests after each brush

BUILT FOR KIDS
- No reading required — voice announcements guide everything
- Large, easy-to-tap buttons designed for small hands
- No ads, no in-app purchases, ever
- Optional front camera detects real brushing motion
- Parent settings protected by a simple math gate

SMART FEATURES
- Adjustable timer per zone (15s, 20s, or 30s)
- Cloud save with Google Sign-In (optional)
- Works completely offline
- Streak tracking to build the daily habit
- Boss battles every 5th session

Brush Quest makes the daily 2-minute routine something kids actually look forward to. No nagging needed — just hand them the phone and let the adventure begin!

Made with love by a dad who was tired of the toothbrushing battle.
